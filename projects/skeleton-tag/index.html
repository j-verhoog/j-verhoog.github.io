Skeleton Tag

Use your face to play this real-time tag game:

  • Position yourself so your head and shoulders fill the frame.
  • Click Start Game to begin (turn on sound as well!).
  • Collect green dots with your nose: +1 point.
  • Avoid red tags: touching them costs 1 point.
  • Other face landmarks (eyes, ears) are in light blue—they’re just for show.
  • Win at 20 points.
  • Note: winning in superfast will unveil a secret

Disclaimer: Webcam access is required but no video or images are recorded, stored, or transmitted. Everything runs locally.
